Air-Tec Prints Website

  • Category: Website
  • Project date: September 2020
  • Project URL: www.airtecperforms.com/prints

A website to provide clients with their property's prints

At the conclusion of a project, Air-Tec creates documents that outline and show the work that they have completed. These documents are always distributed to the general contractor, but are often misplaced. For VIP clients, Air-Tec provides every instance of these As-Built files created, and a portal to access them. This portal divides the documents into seperate pages based off of Building Address, document type, and project name.

The slides are as follows:

  1. Login Page
  2. List of Avalible Buildings
  3. Selection of Document Types
  4. List of completed projects, sorted by completion
  5. Desired Document (opened in new tab)
