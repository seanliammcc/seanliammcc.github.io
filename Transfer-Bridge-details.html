Transfer Bridge Modeling

  • Category: Workshop
  • Project date: September 5th, 2020

Solidworks Modeling & Assembly Workshop

To help community college students transition to UCLA, the Transfer Bridge Program equips all participants with the tools needed to succeed. These skills include Python, Matlab, Linear Algebra, and SolidWorks. I delivered two workshops designed to assist our participants hone their modeling skills. In the first, they created a basic bais bar, and in the second, a racecar.
