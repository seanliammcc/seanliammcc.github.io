The Lab

I'm starting a lab in the department of Cognitive Science at the University of California, Irvine!

The lab's ongoing research investigates the influence of memories on behavior. Specific projects in progress examine the roles of episodic and working memory in decisions for reward, perceptual inference, drug choice and addiction, and intertemporal choice, using computational model-driven analysis of behavior and brain activity (via fMRI, but with plans for EEG and other methods).

We're hiring! If you're interested in working in the lab - as a research assistant, lab manager, or postdoc - please send me a brief (~2 paragraph) message describing your research interests. Include a CV and email addresses of up to three references. Postdoc candidates should include two or three relevant manuscripts (preprints are ok).

Looking to do a PhD? Applications for the Fall 2019 Cognitive Science PhD cohort are closed, but if you're thinking of applying for 2020, I'm happy to talk and see if you might be a good fit. Inquiries should include the above information (summary, CV, references, manuscripts if any).
